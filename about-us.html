About Us

Welcome to the International Youth Fellowship (IYF) Thika, a vibrant hub dedicated to shaping the leaders of tomorrow. Rooted in a global mindset and driven by a local passion for community transformation, we empower young people in Kiambu County and beyond to break past their limitations and discover their true potential.
We believe that true change doesn't just happen on the outside—it begins within the heart.

Our Core Pillars

Our approach is built on three foundational values that guide everything we do:

Challenge: Encouraging youth to step out of their comfort zones, overcome limitations, and discover endless possibilities.

Change: Fostering a genuine transformation of the heart that naturally overflows into positive action within society.

Cohesion: Cultivating a united mind that transcends tribal, national, religious, and cultural boundaries to bring people together.

What We Do

1. Mindset Education

The root of many challenges facing modern youth—such as self-indulgence, isolation, and a lack of direction—stems from a lack of strong inner leadership. Through our world-renowned Mindset Education programs, we teach emotional intelligence, self-control, and deep thinking, equipping young minds with the resilience needed to navigate life's pressures.

2. The Free Weekend Academy

We regularly organize international fellowships, youth camps, and cultural events. By exposing our youth to global perspectives, music, and performance arts, we nurture global citizens who are ready to lead on an international stage.

Our Vision

To be a center of light and transformation in Thika, raising a self-reliant, morally upright, and sound-hearted generation that actively contributes to the progress of Kenya and the African continent.
